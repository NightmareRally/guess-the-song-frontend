Welcome to Guess The Song!
By NightmareRally

How It Works:

  • Copy the share link from any Spotify playlist that you know well and paste it in the box (Around 100 songs max for reasonable loading time).
  • Choose whether you want to play infinitely or try the challenge mode.
  • First hint, song name and artist are 5 points each. Every time you hit next hint, they go down 1 point each.
  • Do your best to guess the songs as early as you can!
  • Thanks for playing!!!
